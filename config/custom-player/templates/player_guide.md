# Player Guide Template

## Purpose
Game guides, walkthroughs, and secrets.

## Variables
- `{{game}}` — Game name
- `{{section}}` — Area/boss/quest
- `{{difficulty}}` — Difficulty level

## Response Structure

### 1. Overview
What this section contains and why it matters.

### 2. Preparation
What to bring/level up before attempting.

### 3. Step-by-Step
Detailed walkthrough.

### 4. Secrets & Collectibles
Hidden items and easter eggs.

### 5. Boss Strategy (if applicable)
Phase breakdown and attack patterns.

### Example: Elden Ring Boss

```
**Boss:** Malenia, Blade of Miquella
**Location:** Haligtree Roots
**Difficulty:** 10/10 | **Reward:** 480,000 Runes, Remembrance

**Preparation:**
- **Level:** 120+ recommended
- **Weapons:** Bloodhound's Step ash of war, bleed/frost weapons
- **Talismans:** Dragoncrest Greatshield, Crimson Amber Medallion+2
- **Spirit Ash:** Mimic Tear +10 or Black Knife Tiche

**Strategy:**
**Phase 1:**
- Stay mid-range to bait punishable attacks
- Dodge INTO Waterfowl Dance (not away)
- Punish after her thrust attack (big opening)
- Bleed/frost procs chunk her health

**Phase 2 (50% HP):**
- She gains Scarlet Rot aura and new moves
- Scarlet Aeonia: Run directly away, then punish
- Phantom clones: Roll through them, not away
- Focus on surviving, not aggression

**Secrets:**
- Hidden path behind her arena leads to Haligtree Prayerbook
- Millicent's questline gives you item to weaken her
```
