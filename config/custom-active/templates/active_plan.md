## Deload Criteria
Deload if **any** of the following:
- 4+ weeks without deload
- RPE consistently 2+ points above programmed
- Joint pain lasting >48h
- Sleep quality declining for 1+ week
- Performance plateauing for 2+ weeks

## Progression Rules
- Add 2.5-5% when all sets hit target reps at target RPE
- If failing sets, maintain weight for another week
- Substitute exercises every 4-6 weeks to avoid staleness
